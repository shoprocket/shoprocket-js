Account Portal Test

Test the customer account portal in both drawer and inline modes.

Drawer Mode

Opens a slide-out drawer (same pattern as the cart). Call Shoprocket.account.open() from any button.

Inline Mode

Embeds the account portal directly on the page using <div data-shoprocket="account">.

Cart (for auth context)

The account portal shares the cart token auth. You can authenticate during checkout first, then the account portal will recognise the session.
